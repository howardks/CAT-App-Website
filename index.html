The New CAT-App!

The Chatham Area Transit mobile application has been completely revamped to improve usability, functionality, and user experience. The new and improved mobile app has all the features you need to easily use the Chatham Area Transit bus system.


Features

Account System

Our secure login system allows users to keep track of their account balance and transaction history. Soon, you will even be able to pay for your bus ride directly from your phone!

Routes

All bus routes can be viewed directly from the app. There's no need to search for them, the Routes page is easily accessible from the app's hamburger menu!

Fares

All fares can be viewed directly from the app as well. Each account type's fare amount is easily accessible from the Fares page in the hamburger menu!

LiveMap

Using our LiveMap feature, you can view where the bus you are waiting for is located in real time!
